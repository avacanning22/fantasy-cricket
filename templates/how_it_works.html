What is it?

The LCC women’s section fantasy league is a summer fundraiser for 2026. It is open to players, friends, and family who want to support the club and follow the women’s section throughout the season.

Like any fantasy league, you earn points when your selected players perform well. The difference here is that your fantasy team is made up of our own club players, not professionals from outside competitions.

How does it work?

There will be four drafts across the summer, aligned with the four Cricket Leinster starring lists.

Each month, you select five players: one from each team in the section and one captain from any team. Your captain scores double points.

If you miss the monthly draft window, a random team will be assigned to keep you in the game.
