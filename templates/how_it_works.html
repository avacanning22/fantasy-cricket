Fantasy Cricket League

What are We?

Summer 2026, LCC's women section is running a Fantasy Cricket League for players, friends and family to support the club and follow the section's progress.

Fantasy cricket is just like any other fantasy league, you'll pick teams at the beginning of every month and if your selected players do well, you'll earn points.

The twist is: the players you pick won't be professional players in leagues or tournaments — it will be our very own club players.

How does it Work?

For summer 2026 there will be 4 drafts that align with the 4 Cricket Leinster starting lists.

You will pick 5 players: one from each team in the section and one captain from any team of your choosing.

How to Join?

To enter the game you pay a once-off entry fee of €30. This can be Revoluted to Jenny Sparrow (Revtag @jennysparrow). This money will all go to help fund the women's section over the summer.

When you sign up you will be asked to provide your full name and phone number.

You will be added to a private WhatsApp group that will receive two texts per month:
1. "DRAFT NOW OPEN FOR 1 WEEK"
2. "DRAFT CLOSING AT MIDNIGHT TONIGHT".

This is a reminder to select your team for the next month. If a team is not selected for the month in the draft window, a random team will be assigned to you to keep you in the game.

How do I Win?

Pick the players who earn the most points. There are points for runs, wickets, catches, stumpings, good economies and loads more.

There will be a table of highest point earners published each month.

The winner of the Fantasy League gets €200 prize!
